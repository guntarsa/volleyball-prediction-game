Scoring System
Match Predictions:
  • 6 points: Perfect prediction (exact sets: 3-0, 3-1, 3-2)
  • 4 points: Correct winner, off by 1 set
  • 2 points: Correct winner only
  • 1 point: Correct total sets, wrong winner
Tournament Predictions:
  • 50 points: Tournament winner
  • 25 points: Runner-up (2nd place)
  • 25 points: Bronze medalist (3rd place)
